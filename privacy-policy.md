# Privacy Policy — ChatGPT Exporter

Last updated: March 2025

ChatGPT Exporter does not collect, store, or transmit any personal 
data, conversation content, or usage information to any external server.

All processing occurs locally within your browser. Conversation data 
is held temporarily in Chrome local storage only for the duration of 
an export (seconds), then deleted automatically.

The extension does not use analytics, tracking pixels, or any 
third-party services.
